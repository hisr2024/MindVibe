# App Store Assets Specification — Kiaanverse

## Android (Google Play)

### App Icon
- **Size**: 512 x 512 px
- **Format**: PNG (32-bit, with alpha)
- **Shape**: Full bleed square (Google applies adaptive icon mask)
- **Design notes**: Kiaanverse lotus/divine symbol on gradient background (saffron-to-deep-purple). Keep core element within safe zone (center 66%). No text — icon should be recognizable at 48x48px.

### Feature Graphic
- **Size**: 1024 x 500 px
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG (24-bit, no alpha)
- **Design notes**: Hero composition with app name "Kiaanverse", tagline "Your Divine Spiritual Companion", subtle sacred geometry patterns, cosmic background with golden particles. Do NOT include device frames — Google overlays them. Keep text in center safe zone (800 x 400 area) to avoid cropping.

### Phone Screenshots
- **Size**: 1080 x 1920 px (minimum) — recommended 1080 x 2340 px (19.5:9)
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG
- **Count**: 4-8 screenshots (minimum 4, recommended 8)
- **Orientation**: Portrait
- **Design notes**: Device frame optional. Use captions above/below the UI screenshot. Brand-consistent background (dark cosmic theme or warm saffron gradient).

### Tablet Screenshots (7-inch)
- **Size**: 1200 x 1920 px (minimum)
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG
- **Count**: 4-8 screenshots
- **Orientation**: Portrait or Landscape
- **Design notes**: Same content as phone, adapted for tablet aspect ratio. Larger text readable.

### Tablet Screenshots (10-inch)
- **Size**: 1920 x 1200 px (minimum, landscape) or 1200 x 1920 px (portrait)
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG
- **Count**: 4-8 screenshots
- **Design notes**: Showcase tablet-optimized layouts with more visible detail.

## iOS (Apple App Store)

### App Icon
- **Size**: 1024 x 1024 px
- **Format**: PNG (no alpha channel, no transparency)
- **Shape**: Full square (Apple applies rounded corners automatically)
- **Color space**: sRGB or Display P3
- **Design notes**: Same design language as Android icon, but ensure no transparency/alpha. Verify corners look good when Apple rounds them (don't place critical elements in corner areas — keep within ~820x820 center safe zone).

### iPhone Screenshots (6.5-inch — required)
- **Device**: iPhone 14 Plus, iPhone 13 Pro Max, iPhone 12 Pro Max, iPhone 11 Pro Max
- **Size**: 1284 x 2778 px (or 1242 x 2688 px)
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG
- **Count**: Up to 10 (minimum 3, recommended 6-8)
- **Orientation**: Portrait

### iPhone Screenshots (5.5-inch — required)
- **Device**: iPhone 8 Plus, iPhone 7 Plus, iPhone 6s Plus
- **Size**: 1242 x 2208 px
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG
- **Count**: Up to 10 (minimum 3, recommended 6-8)
- **Orientation**: Portrait
- **Design notes**: Can reuse 6.5" designs scaled down, but verify text remains legible.

### iPad Screenshots (12.9-inch — required if supporting iPad)
- **Device**: iPad Pro 12.9" (3rd gen and later)
- **Size**: 2048 x 2732 px
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG
- **Count**: Up to 10 (minimum 3, recommended 5)
- **Orientation**: Portrait or Landscape
- **Design notes**: Showcase tablet layout. Larger canvas allows more detail and text.

### App Preview Videos (optional but recommended)
- **iPhone 6.5"**: 886 x 1920 px or 1920 x 886 px (H.264, 15-30 seconds)
- **iPad 12.9"**: 1200 x 1600 px (H.264, 15-30 seconds)
- **Content**: Show Sakha AI conversation, Sadhana flow, mood check-in, Gita browsing

## Shared Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Deep Cosmic Purple | #1A0533 | Primary backgrounds |
| Sacred Saffron | #FF9933 | Accents, CTAs |
| Divine Gold | #FFD700 | Highlights, particles |
| Lotus Pink | #E91E8C | Mood/emotional elements |
| Serene White | #FAFAFA | Text, clean surfaces |
| Krishna Blue | #1E3A5F | Secondary accents |

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Crimson Text (serif) — spiritual, timeless feel
- **Body**: System default (SF Pro on iOS, Roboto on Android)
- **Sanskrit**: Noto Sans Devanagari

### Screenshot Caption Style
- Position: Top 20% of screenshot frame
- Font: Bold, 28-36pt equivalent
- Color: White on dark / Dark on light
- Keep to 5-7 words max per caption
- Examples: "Meet Sakha, Your Divine Guide" / "Track Your Inner Journey"
