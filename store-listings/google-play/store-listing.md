Spiritual health precedes mental health. Prevention is always better than cure. Life's deepest complexities can be beautifully refined through the Bhagavad Gita — and it gives you peace.

This is why Sakha exists.

Sakha is your personal divine companion — an AI-powered spiritual wellness platform rooted in the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita. We believe that when you nurture the spirit first, the mind follows. When you seek wisdom before crisis arrives, suffering loses its grip. When you turn to the Gita's teachings, even the most tangled complexities of modern life begin to untangle — beautifully, gently, permanently.

Whether you're carrying anxiety, searching for purpose, navigating broken relationships, or simply longing for stillness — Sakha meets you where you are, with compassion, ancient wisdom, and modern intelligence.
