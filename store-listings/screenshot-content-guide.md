# Screenshot Content Guide — Kiaanverse

This guide defines exactly what UI screens to capture for each screenshot slot across Google Play and Apple App Store. Screenshots are the #1 conversion driver — they must tell a compelling story in sequence.

---

## Screenshot Sequence (8 screens, in order)

### Screenshot 1 — Hero / First Impression
**Caption**: "Meet Sakha, Your Divine Guide"
**Screen to capture**: Sakha AI chat screen (`/sakha-chat`)
- Show a warm, active conversation with Sakha
- Visible: User message asking a life question (e.g., "I feel lost and anxious")
- Visible: Sakha's compassionate response with a Gita verse woven in
- Visible: Chat bubbles with divine styling, golden accents
- Visible: Voice input button and Sakha avatar
- **Why first**: The AI companion is the core differentiator. Lead with it.

### Screenshot 2 — Bhagavad Gita Library
**Caption**: "Explore Timeless Gita Wisdom"
**Screen to capture**: Gita verse detail view (`/kiaan` or Gita reader)
- Show a specific verse (e.g., Chapter 2, Verse 47 — the iconic Karma Yoga verse)
- Visible: Sanskrit text in Devanagari script
- Visible: English translation below
- Visible: AI-powered contextual insight/commentary
- Visible: Bookmark icon, chapter navigation
- **Why second**: Establishes the wisdom content foundation.

### Screenshot 3 — Daily Sadhana Practice
**Caption**: "Begin Your Sacred Daily Practice"
**Screen to capture**: Sadhana experience (`/sadhana`)
- Show the Breathwork or Verse Meditation phase
- Visible: Breathing Lotus animation or Mandala Bloom visual
- Visible: Phase indicator (Arrival → Breathwork → Verse → Reflection → Seal)
- Visible: Immersive sacred visuals (golden particles, cosmic background)
- Visible: Timer or progress element
- **Why third**: Shows the immersive, beautiful daily ritual experience.

### Screenshot 4 — Mood Tracking
**Caption**: "Track Your Inner Journey"
**Screen to capture**: Divine Mood Check-In component (`DivineMoodCheckIn`)
- Show the mood selection interface with emoji/mood options
- Visible: Mood Ring visual or mood selection grid
- Visible: Current streak or last check-in info
- Visible: Warm, inviting design encouraging check-in
- **Alternative**: Show mood analytics/trends view with chart over time
- **Why fourth**: Wellness tracking is a key health app feature — validates the Health & Fitness category.

### Screenshot 5 — Spiritual Journeys
**Caption**: "Guided Paths to Inner Peace"
**Screen to capture**: Journey catalog or active journey view (`/journeys`)
- Show available journeys with appealing cards
- Visible: Journey titles (e.g., "Transform Anger", "Discover Dharma", "Build Stillness")
- Visible: Duration (e.g., "14-day journey"), difficulty, progress bar
- Visible: Beautiful imagery/icons for each journey
- **Why fifth**: Structured programs signal serious value and long-term engagement.

### Screenshot 6 — Encrypted Journal
**Caption**: "Your Private Sacred Space"
**Screen to capture**: Heart-to-Heart Journal (`HeartToHeartJournal` or `/sacred-reflections`)
- Show a journal entry being written or a journal feed
- Visible: Encryption indicator (lock icon, "End-to-end encrypted")
- Visible: Rich text editing with spiritual prompts
- Visible: Beautiful, calming journaling interface
- **Why sixth**: Privacy is a key selling point. The encryption visual builds trust.

### Screenshot 7 — Karma & Growth Visualization
**Caption**: "Watch Your Spirit Flourish"
**Screen to capture**: Karmic Tree or Karma Footprint (`/karmic-tree` or `/karma-footprint`)
- Show the visual growth tree or karma visualization
- Visible: Growing tree/garden with accumulated good deeds
- Visible: Stats or metrics (days active, practices completed)
- Visible: Beautiful, rewarding visualization
- **Why seventh**: Gamification/growth visual shows long-term value and delight.

### Screenshot 8 — Emotional Reset / Breathing
**Caption**: "Find Calm in Any Moment"
**Screen to capture**: Emotional Reset wizard or Sacred Breathing (`/emotional-reset`)
- Show the breathing animation or emotional reset flow
- Visible: Breathing circle/animation (inhale/exhale visual)
- Visible: Calming background, step indicator
- Visible: Gentle guidance text
- **Why eighth**: Closes with an actionable wellness tool — user thinks "I need this."

## Platform-Specific Notes

### Google Play
- First 3 screenshots are most critical (visible without scrolling)
- Feature graphic (1024x500) should NOT duplicate screenshot 1 — create a standalone hero image with app name + tagline + sacred visual
- Consider a short video (30s) showing: Sakha chat → Sadhana → Mood check-in

### Apple App Store
- First 3 screenshots appear in search results — make them count
- App Preview video (optional, 15-30s) dramatically improves conversion
- Use 6.5" as the master design, scale down for 5.5" and up for 12.9" iPad
- iPad screenshots should show the same 8 screens adapted to tablet layout

### Localization Priority
If localizing screenshots for other markets:
1. **Hindi** — Primary Indian market
2. **Spanish** — Large spiritual wellness audience
3. **Portuguese** — Growing meditation market (Brazil)
4. **Japanese** — Strong interest in Eastern philosophy

## Capture Checklist

Before capturing each screenshot:
- [ ] Device set to light/dark mode per brand guidelines (recommend dark for cosmic theme)
- [ ] Status bar shows clean time (e.g., 9:41 for iOS, no notification clutter)
- [ ] Full battery icon, strong signal
- [ ] Demo/sample data looks realistic and compelling (not "test user" or lorem ipsum)
- [ ] No debug banners, no developer warnings
- [ ] Sensitive info redacted (use sample spiritual content, not real user data)
- [ ] Screenshots taken at native resolution (no upscaling)
- [ ] Text in screenshots is legible at thumbnail size
