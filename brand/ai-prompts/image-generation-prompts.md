## Generation Tips

### Color Accuracy
Always include hex codes in prompts for precise brand colors:
- Orange Primary: `#FF6B35`
- Gold: `#FFD700`
- Teal: `#00CED1` or `#4FD1C5`
- Deep Blue: `#1E40AF`
- Rose: `#FF6B9D`

### Style Consistency
Add these modifiers for brand consistency:
- `--stylize 500-750` for controlled creativity
- `--v 6` for latest Midjourney quality
- `premium quality`, `professional brand identity`
- `minimal design`, `modern aesthetic`

### Aspect Ratios
- Icons: `--ar 1:1`
- Wordmarks: `--ar 4:1` or `--ar 5:1`
- Feathers: `--ar 1:2`
- UI Concepts: `--ar 1:1` or `--ar 16:9`

### Negative Prompts (if supported)
```
--no text, words, letters, watermark, signature, religious symbols, busy patterns, cartoon style, clip art
```
