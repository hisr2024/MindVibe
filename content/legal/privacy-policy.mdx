## 1. Introduction {#introduction}

Sakha ("we", "our", "us") operates the MindVibe spiritual wellness platform and
the Sakha mobile and web applications (together, the "Service"). We treat your
data as sacred. This Privacy Policy explains — in plain language — what we
collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and the rights you have to
inspect, export, correct, or erase it.

We comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California
Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), the Apple App Store privacy requirements, and
the Google Play Data Safety rules. If local law grants you more rights than
this policy describes, those local rights prevail.

## 2. Data We Collect {#data-we-collect}

We deliberately collect the minimum data required to guide your spiritual
journey. We do not sell your data. We never share your reflections, journals,
or emotional state with advertisers.

### 2.1 Account Data {#account-data}

When you create an account we collect an email address, a hashed password (or
an OAuth identifier from Apple or Google), and a display name. Email is used
for essential service messages only.

### 2.2 Journey & Reflection Data {#journey-data}

Your journal entries, reflections, Gita verse bookmarks, and journey progress
are stored encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM using a key derived from your
passphrase. We cannot read this content.

### 2.3 Emotional State Data {#emotional-data}

Mood tags, emotional resets, and KIAAN conversation transcripts are treated as
the highest privacy tier. They are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest,
and are never used to train public AI models.

### 2.4 Technical Data {#technical-data}

We collect device type, operating system, app version, crash reports, and
anonymized performance metrics. Crash reports are scrubbed of personal data
before upload.

### 2.5 Data We Explicitly Do Not Collect {#never-collect}

| Category | Collected | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Advertising identifiers (IDFA, AAID) | No | Never read, never stored. |
| Precise location (GPS) | No | Only coarse timezone, for daily reminders. |
| Contacts | No | Sakha never asks for your address book. |
| Photos, microphone, camera | Only with consent | Used only for voice companion and profile picture, when you enable them. |
| Browsing history | No | We do not track you across other sites or apps. |

## 3. How We Use Your Data {#how-we-use}

We use the data described above for the following lawful purposes.

### 3.1 Providing the Service {#providing}

We process your account, journey progress, and reflections so you can continue
your practice across devices. Legal basis: performance of contract
(GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).

### 3.2 Improving the Service {#improving}

We use aggregated, anonymized metrics to understand feature adoption and to
fix bugs. Legal basis: legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).

### 3.3 Sending Essential Communications {#communications}

We send security alerts, password resets, and critical service notices. You
cannot opt out of these. Marketing emails are strictly opt-in and separate.

## 4. How We Protect Your Data {#protection}

### 4.1 Encryption {#encryption}

All journal and reflection content is encrypted client-side with AES-256-GCM
before it leaves your device. Keys are derived via PBKDF2 with 600,000
iterations from your passphrase. We never see your passphrase.

### 4.2 Infrastructure {#infrastructure}

Production services run in SOC 2 Type II audited data centers. Database
connections are TLS 1.3 only. Secrets are stored in a managed vault; no
developer has direct access to user content.

### 4.3 Incident Response {#incident-response}

If we suffer a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you
and the relevant authorities within 72 hours, as required by GDPR Art. 33
and 34.

## 5. Your Rights {#your-rights}

You have the following rights over your data. We respond to every verified
request within 30 days at no cost.

### 5.1 Access and Portability {#access}

You may download a complete machine-readable export of your account,
journeys, reflections, and emotional history at any time from
Settings → Privacy → Export My Data.

### 5.2 Correction {#correction}

You may correct inaccurate personal data directly in the app, or by writing
to us at privacy@sakha.ai.

### 5.3 Erasure (Right to Be Forgotten) {#erasure}

Settings → Privacy → Delete My Account permanently removes your data within
30 days. Backups are purged within 90 days. Some anonymized aggregated
metrics may be retained for integrity and security audits.

### 5.4 Objection and Restriction {#objection}

You may object to processing based on legitimate interests, or ask us to
restrict processing while a dispute is resolved.

### 5.5 California Rights (CCPA/CPRA) {#california}

California residents have the right to know, delete, correct, and opt-out of
sale or sharing of personal information. Sakha does not sell or share
personal information, so the opt-out is automatically honored. You may also
designate an authorized agent to exercise these rights on your behalf.

## 6. Data Sharing {#data-sharing}

We share data only in the narrow circumstances listed below. We never sell
your data.

| Recipient | Purpose | Data Shared |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cloud infrastructure providers | Hosting and backups | Encrypted blobs only |
| Payment processors (Stripe, Apple, Google) | Process subscriptions | Billing details — never journal content |
| Email delivery (transactional only) | Send password resets and receipts | Email address, message contents |
| Legal authorities | Only when legally compelled | Minimum necessary, with notice when allowed |

## 7. International Transfers {#international}

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland
and your data is transferred to the United States, we rely on Standard
Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission and on supplementary
safeguards. You may request a copy of the clauses at privacy@sakha.ai.

## 8. Children's Privacy {#children}

Sakha is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect
personal data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected
data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. Parents may contact
us at privacy@sakha.ai.

## 9. Platform-Specific Disclosures {#platform-disclosures}

### 9.1 Apple App Store {#apple}

Per Apple's Nutrition Label requirements, Sakha links the following data
categories to your identity only when you sign in: contact info (email),
user content (journals, reflections), usage data (app interactions), and
diagnostics (crash logs). No data is used for tracking.

### 9.2 Google Play {#google-play}

Per the Google Play Data Safety form, Sakha collects personal info, app
activity, and app info and performance. All data is encrypted in transit.
You can request deletion at Settings → Privacy → Delete My Account.

## 10. Changes to This Policy {#changes}

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. We
will notify you in the app at least 30 days before material changes take
effect. Historical versions are available at sakha.ai/privacy/history.

## 11. Contact Us {#contact}

For any privacy question, contact our Data Protection Officer at
dpo@sakha.ai, or by post at Sakha, Privacy Office, 1 Serene Way, San
Francisco, CA 94107, USA. If you are in the EU, you may also lodge a
